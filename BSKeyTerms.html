The key terms carry an average of 16 marks in Paper-1, accounting for 10% of your total marks and thus, could easily differentiate between A and A*. *Tip*

When answering the key terms in our website, you can check the answer by pressing ENTER Key. This will automatically check your answer and take you to the next question if your answer is correct.

Our Marking System

A maximum of 4 keywords and a minimum of 1 keyword are chosen with sincere consideration for each key terms and your inputted answers will be checked for the presence of those keyword(s). The chronological order of these keywords does not matter. All definitions of the keywords are endorsed from Business Studies Coursebook(ISBN: 9781107680258).
